# Creator Guide

My Creator Hub shows participation, Thursday POP, Away Notice, availability, personal
standing, and timeline. Only your own permitted information is visible.

Meaningful participation is real conversation in an enabled Main Group topic. Photos,
stickers, greetings, emoji-only posts, filler, promotions, and repeated text do not count.
Use an Away Notice when time away should pause expectations; private details are optional.
Submit POP only through the configured Sellers Group POP workflow.
