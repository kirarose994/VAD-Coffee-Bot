# Creator Guide

My VAD Home shows participation, Thursday POP, Away Notice, availability, personal
standing, and timeline. Only your own permitted information is visible.

Meaningful participation is real conversation in an enabled Main Group topic. Photos,
stickers, greetings, emoji-only posts, filler, promotions, and repeated text do not count.
The goal is to add value through discussion, thoughtful responses, questions, or help—not
simply to post every two days. This keeps the community lively and gives members a reason to
come back.
Use an Away Notice when time away should pause expectations; private details are optional.
Submit POP only through the configured Sellers Group POP workflow.

Contact an Admin creates a private support request after preview and confirmation. My Support
Requests shows only requests tied to your immutable Telegram ID.
